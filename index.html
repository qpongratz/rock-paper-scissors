Quartz, Parchment, Shears

Step right up and try your hand at a game passed down from my grandmother. It's quite simple. Merely select one of these instruments below and win or lose based on a few basic rules.

  • Quartz (fig. 1) beats Shears (fig. 3)
  • Parchment (fig. 2) beats Quartz (fig. 1)
  • Shears (fig. 3) beats Parchment (fig. 2)
The twist is that this here version is played against a robot. First to five points wins. Good Luck!
